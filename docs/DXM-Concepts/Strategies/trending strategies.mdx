# Trending Strategy

<details>
<summary><strong>Overview</strong></summary>
<p><strong>Name of the Strategy:</strong> Trending</p>
<p><strong>Short Description:</strong> The Trending Products model identifies and recommends products that are currently popular or gaining significant attention among users. It leverages real-time data and trend analysis to highlight items that are trending in the market, ensuring customers are aware of the latest popular products.</p>
<p><strong>How it works:</strong> The Trending Products model leverages advanced algorithms to analyze multiple data sources and identify emerging trends. The methodology includes:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Trend Detection:</strong> Uses time-series analysis, clustering, and sentiment analysis to identify patterns and trends.</li>
  <li><strong>Scoring and Ranking:</strong> Scores products based on recent sales spikes, increased user engagement, and positive sentiment on social media. The products are then ranked according to their scores.</li>
  <li><strong>Recommendation Engine:</strong> The top-ranked products are recommended to users through various touch points on the retail website.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Input Requirements:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Sales Data:</strong> Historical and real-time sales figures.</li>
  <li><strong>User Interactions:</strong> Clicks, views, add to carts, purchase history.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Output:</strong> A ranked list of trending products.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Business Impact</strong></summary>
<p><strong>Goal:</strong> To boost sales and customer engagement by recommending trending products, enhancing the shopping experience.</p>
<p><strong>Business Metrics Impacted:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Engagement Metrics:</strong> Enhanced user engagement, such as click-through rates and time spent on site.</li>
  <li><strong>Customer Bounce Rate:</strong> Reduce the number of users bouncing off from the website.</li>
  <li><strong>Conversion Rate:</strong> Higher likelihood of purchases.</li>
  <li><strong>Sales Volume:</strong> Increase in sales of trending products.</li>
  <li><strong>Average Order Value (AOV):</strong> Increased AOV through cross-selling.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Success Stories:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Etsy:</strong> Etsy utilizes a Trending Products model to highlight popular and seasonal items on its platform. This strategy has led to significant sales boosts during key shopping periods, like holidays.</li>
  <li><strong>Zalando:</strong> Zalando uses the model to stay ahead of fashion trends, resulting in a 15% sales increase of featured products.</li>
</ul>
</details>
